Who are we?

Gratiiam™ is a South African luxury watch brand which aims to instill confidence and inspiration through its distinguished time pieces. Even though Gratiiam™ makes elegant time pieces, it is beyond just looking good, it is about feeling good and confident when wearing it. Our aim is for everyone who wears the Gratiiam™ watch to feel and know that anything is possible just by looking at their wrist. We aim to birth African and worldwide excellence through our watches.

WE ARE AN ETERNAL AFRICAN EXCELLENCY!

Growing up on the African continent, many of us perceived the world beyond our borders as distant and unattainable, accessible only through the limited view of a screen. We often viewed certain achievements, such as building a large brand capable of competing with major international corporations, performing in or producing a blockbuster film, or becoming a globally recognized artist like the celebrities we admired, as unfeasible and reserved for a select few.

However, the passing of time has revealed that the impossible can become reality. With African actors, designers, musicians, and entrepreneurs breaking down such barriers, we have come to realize the boundless possibilities and opportunities that exist. Our luxury brand was born from a desire to leverage these newfound opportunities and inspire confidence and motivation in African creatives and dreamers. We aim to see more African individuals pursue their aspirations, triumph over the world, and believe in their abilities. As an Eternal African Excellency, we create products that facilitate this objective.
